WOKAB Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

Overview

WOKAB is a mobile application that helps you learn English vocabulary. We respect your privacy and take all necessary measures to protect your personal data.

Data Collected

WOKAB is a fully offline-first application. The following data is stored only locally on your device:

  • Your study history (session statistics, streak records)
  • Your vocabulary learning progress (known/learning words)
  • App preferences (theme, daily goal, notification settings)
  • Your nickname (optional, stored only on your device)

This data is never sent to any server and is stored only on your device.

Third-Party Services

WOKAB uses the following:

  • Expo Push Notifications: Expo's push notification service is used to send reminders. When notifications are enabled, your device push token is sent to Expo servers. No other personal data is shared.

WOKAB does not use any advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties.

Data Security

All your data is stored in an encrypted SQLite database in your device's local storage. When you delete the app, all data is permanently removed.

Children's Privacy

WOKAB is suitable for ages 4+. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Significant changes will be announced via in-app notifications.
